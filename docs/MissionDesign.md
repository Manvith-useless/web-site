# Mission Design — Vertical Slice: The Awakening

## Fantasy
Infiltrate a clandestine lab, exfiltrate a Meridan artifact, and decide whether to spare or eliminate a key witness—establishing stealth, disguise, and Echo Blade combat.

## Beat-by-Beat
1. Arrival: rooftop drop-off; optional recon drone vista
2. Entry: guard rotation; disguise acquisition tutorial (shape-shift)
3. Restricted Zone: badge checks; social stealth and suspicion meters
4. Artifact Vault: puzzle-lite power reroute; optional stealth takedowns
5. Alarm Trigger (branch):
   - Silent path: ghost objective; disable uplink
   - Loud path: combat tutorial; Echo Blades combos + grapples
6. Mini-Boss: Meridan Enforcer; teaches dodge timing and stagger windows
7. Choice: Spare/Eliminate Witness → morality delta; unlocks next mission variant
8. Extraction: elevator collapse set-piece; timed escape across unstable catwalks

## Objectives
- Primary: Retrieve artifact; extract
- Secondary: Stay undetected or neutralize < 3 enemies; collect intel cache

## Encounter Design
- Patrol cones, light-based visibility, distraction props
- Enemy archetypes: Guard (pistol), Enforcer (baton/shield), Technician (panic/alarm)

## Tutorialization
- On-screen prompts for disguise, takedown, combo finishers, and parry timing

## Co-op Variant
- Duo takedowns; synchronized distract-and-stealth windows; shared objective widgets

## Metrics Targets
- 12–18 minute clear; 60–90 second combat arena; 30–45 second set-piece escape
