# Morality, Choices, and Branching Endings

## Morality Model
- Two axes tracked per profile: Justice (Mercy ↔ Retribution) and Integrity (Honesty ↔ Deception).
- System Inputs: interrogation outcomes, civilian risk tolerance, disguise abuse, collateral damage, and finishing move types.
- Outputs: faction reputation, unique missions, gear unlocks, and ending eligibility.

## Choice Design Principles
- Short-term utility vs long-term narrative impact; telegraph consequences via NPC reactions and world-state changes.
- Avoid pure “good/evil”; present trade-offs and ambiguous intelligence.
- Co-op: consensus or split—if split, branch uses host preference but records dissent for finale modifiers.

## Key Branch Gates
1. Mission 1 Interrogation: execute, arrest, recruit.
2. Midgame Alliance: collaborate with Zero early (Redemption boost) or opportunistically exploit him (Corruption drift).
3. Civilian Evacuations: optional timed objectives; neglect leads to Greed empowerment later.
4. Eclipse Intel Trade: accept shadow pact (power now, cost later) or reject (harder path, cleaner end).

## Ending Matrix (High-Level)
- Redemption Campaign (Cameleon/Zero):
  - True Redemption: high Justice, high Integrity; Devil contained, Greed sealed; Zero frees himself from Eclipse.
  - Burdened Vigilante: high Justice, low Integrity; Devil defeated but collateral scars Earth Prime; Zero estranged.
  - Fractured Peace: mid-range; Devil escapes wounded; Meridan stability protocol holds temporarily.

- Corruption Campaign (Devil/Eclipse):
  - Sovereign of Greed: low Justice, low Integrity; Greed ascendant; cities subjugated via Meridan siphons.
  - Pragmatic Tyrant: low Justice, high Integrity; order through fear; Eclipse retains leash on shadows.
  - Collapse: reckless devouring triggers cataclysm; non-standard game over with epilogue comics.

## System Hooks
- UI: discreet morality glyphs on results screen; no explicit numeric bars.
- Gameplay Tuning: parry windows, stealth suspicion, and Meridan overflow subtly shift with morality tier.
- Narrative: VO, costume variants, safehouse NPC availability respond to axes.
