# Mission 1 — "Ashes of the Contract"

## Premise
On Earth Prime, a routine assassination in a luxury hotel spirals into a Meridan surge. Cameleon must extract, decide the target's fate, and escape as the city district begins to collapse.

## Objectives (Player-Facing)
1. Infiltrate the hotel penthouse floor without raising an alarm.
2. Interrogate the target (black-market Meridan broker) for intel on Devil.
3. Choose: execute, arrest (non-lethal), or recruit as informant.
4. Escape through service routes amid an escalating Meridan event.

## Space & Flow
- Hub: Lobby (social stealth), Staff corridors (restricted), Penthouse (target), Rooftop service exit.
- Multiple entries: valet, service elevator, maintenance shaft.
- Dual-World Echo: micro-phasing anomalies reveal hidden vents and alternate paths momentarily.

## Key Systems Spotlight
- Disguise loop: steal uniform, maintain behavior, pass checkpoints.
- Echo Link tutorial: tether guards to environmental hazards for silent takedowns.
- Meridan instability tutorial: timed hazards, flickering lights, debris phase.

## Encounter Beats
- Lobby: mimic staff routines to avoid suspicion; optional side intel.
- Penthouse: small guard squad, sound-reactive doors, glass reflections as sight risks.
- Escape: vertical chase with collapses; optional co-op split paths.

## Cinematics
- Opening: Cameleon finishing a contract; guilt flicker; Zero observes from afar (554567 relay).
- Mid: Interrogation choice impacts later Devil confrontation modifiers.
- End: Devil teases Greed’s presence; Meridan spike cliffhanger.

## Metrics (Prototype Targets)
- Mission length: 15–25 minutes for stealth-first; 10–15 for aggressive.
- Alarm budgets: 0–1 for S-rank; up to 3 for C-rank.
- Performance: 60 FPS target, <120 AI agents global, <20 active in vicinity.

## Graybox Checklist
- Blockout: lobby, staff floors, penthouse, roof.
- Spline nav for crowd, patrol routes, and evac flows.
- Interactive props: disguises, vents, fuse boxes, echo anchor points.
