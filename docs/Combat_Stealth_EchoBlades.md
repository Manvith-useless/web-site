# Combat, Stealth, and Echo Blades Mechanics

## Combat Overview
- Third-person melee core with stance system: Chain, Edge, Echo.
- Lock-on with soft target priority, manual swap, and camera assist.
- Cancel windows after parries and perfect dodges; stamina-lite via Meridan meter.

## Stances
- Chain Stance: Wide arcs, improved tracking, crowd control; slower recoveries.
- Edge Stance: Short reach, high precision, bleed and stagger; parry-focused.
- Echo Stance: Consumes Meridan; gap closers, Echo Link, projectile reflects, utility.

## Echo Link (Signature Mechanic)
- Tether up to N targets or anchor points.
- Damage routes along links; finisher detonates links for AoE.
- In co-op, both players can contribute links for amplifications.

## Stealth Systems
- Visibility cones, luminance, occlusion, and sound propagation.
- Social Stealth via Disguises: suspicion meter rises from anomalous behavior; proximity checks.
- Tools: noise darts, light disruptors, decoys, grapple assist (late).
- Non-lethal path: choke-chain, zip-tie, stash bodies; mission grades reward restraint.

## Echo Blades Spec
- Primary: Dual chained blades with variable length; retractable to forearms.
- Secondary: Serrated combat knife for stealth kills, finishers, and parry ripostes.
- Mod Slots: chain weight, blade edge, echo capacitors.

## Enemy Archetypes (Initial)
- Patrolman/Guard: sightlines, radios, morale break.
- Enforcer: armor, slow but relentless; punish overextensions.
- Acolyte of Greed: absorbs debris, emits unstable bursts.
- Shadow Adept: manipulates light, punishes camping; counters stealth.

## Co-Op Combat Interactions
- Synchronized takedowns, juggle handoffs, revive i-frames with resource cost.
- Shared Meridan burst: both commit meter to trigger space-warp crowd control.
