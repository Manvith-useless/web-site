# Cameleon: Dual Fate — Game Design Document (GDD)

## Vision Statement
Create a cinematic third-person action-adventure with stealth, high-mobility melee combat, and cooperative play, set across two parallel Earths. Players experience a morally charged narrative where identity, justice, betrayal, and redemption shape gameplay systems and multiple endings.

## Core Pillars
1. Identity as Mechanic: Shape-shifting and moral choices meaningfully alter stealth, social infiltration, and narrative.
2. Blades and Brutality: Echo Blades enable chained, momentum-based melee with stance depth and readable, weighty animation.
3. Dual-World Drama: Earth Prime vs Earth 554567 surface in level design, enemy kits, and mission structure.
4. Cinematic Co-Op: Two-character campaign arcs with complementary abilities and synchronized takedowns.
5. Player-Authored Endings: Systemic morality and campaign selection (Redemption vs Corruption) determine outcomes.

## Setting & Tone
- Modern-day Earth with clandestine conflicts over Meridans—volatile dimensional rifts and energy anomalies.
- A covert arms race between Earth Prime and Earth 554567; splinter cells, black sites, and civilian spaces under siege.
- Tone: Neo-noir techno-thriller with grounded brutality and surreal interludes inside Meridan hotspots.

## Playable Characters (Campaigns)
- Redemption Campaign: Cameleon + Zero
- Corruption Campaign: Devil + Eclipse

Each campaign features unique missions, bespoke cinematics, ability synergies, and distinct endings.

## High-Level Narrative Outline
Act I: Awakening — Cameleon’s last contract collapses into a Meridan incident; Zero is deployed from 554567.
Act II: Fracture — Alliances form; Devil’s pact with Greed escalates; Meridan instability spreads.
Act III: Reckoning — Two climactic boss battles and branching outcomes per morality and campaign.

## Major Set Pieces
- Citywide blackout and Meridan surge during Cameleon vs Devil in a collapsing high-rise district.
- Surreal illusion chamber for Zero vs Eclipse: shifting geometry, shadow hazards, and memory echoes.

## Gameplay Overview
- Third-person, over-the-shoulder camera with cinematic framing and smart lock-on.
- Mobility: vaulting, wall-climb assists, slide, dodge-cancel, grapple hook (late-game upgrade).
- Stealth: social infiltration (shape-shift), classic line-of-sight stealth, light and sound systems, disguise suspicion meter.
- Combat: stance-based Echo Blades melee, contextual executions, non-lethal options, synergy combos in co-op.

## Echo Blades — Intent and Fantasy
- Hybrid of chained dual blades and a serrated combat knife.
- Modes: Chain Stance (range control, crowd management), Edge Stance (precision, single-target burst), Echo Stance (Meridan-charged utilities, parries, and gap-closers).
- Signature: Echo Link—tether multiple enemies/environment points to route damage and movement.

## Co-Op Philosophy
- Drop-in/out 2P online co-op; AI partner in solo.
- Synchronized takedowns, combo extenders, revive and risk-reward shared Meridan resource.
- Complementary roles: Infiltrator (Cameleon/Devil) + Weapons Savant or Controller (Zero/Eclipse).

## Progression & Systems (High-Level)
- Ability Trees: three branches per character emphasizing identity fantasy.
- Gear: Echo Blade mods, knife edges, utility gadgets, and stealth tools.
- Meridan Mastery: risk vs reward meter that powers Echo Stance utilities; instability causes anomalies.
- Reputation & Morality: faction standing unlocks intel, support, and narrative branches.

## Level Design Principles
- Layered spaces: public fronts, restricted interiors, back-channel traversal routes.
- Dual-World Twins: mirrored layouts with divergent hazards/enemies.
- Set-piece triggers tied to Meridan instability and player morality.

## Boss Battles (Concept)
1) Cameleon vs Devil: time-dilated Meridan surges, destructible cover, civilians at risk; morality affects objectives.
2) Zero vs Eclipse: light vs shadow mechanics, weapon mastery counters, illusion puzzles in phases.

## Technical Targets
- Visuals: Target GTA V-level fidelity or better on modern PCs/PS5/Xbox Series.
- Camera: 60 FPS target, motion blur optional, comprehensive photo mode.
- Networking: deterministic combat states for co-op, rollback-inspired reconnection and host migration.

## Accessibility (Initial Pass)
- Remappable inputs, color-blind safe palettes for stealth and Meridan cues, subtitle and UI scaling, aim assist tiers, QTE alternatives.

## Content Rating & Themes
- Mature themes of violence, betrayal, redemption. Option for non-lethal runs.

## Production Snapshot
- Prototype vertical slice: 1 stealth-social mission, 1 combat arena, 1 boss graybox.
- Tooling: modular abilities, state machines, and data-driven AI behaviors.

## Open Questions
- Balance between social stealth and combat density per mission.
- Co-op synchronization windows for Echo Link and takedowns.
- Narrative branch budget and cinematic scope per ending.
