# Narrative Outline — Cameleon: Dual Fate

## Cast
- Cameleon: contract killer turned vigilant awakener; shape-shifter.
- Zero: weapon savant from Earth 554567; seeks truth of his origin.
- Devil: Cameleon’s corrupted friend bound to Greed; energy consumption.
- Eclipse: Zero’s master; manipulates shadows and memory.
- Greed: demonic entity; tempts with power for a price.

## Structure
Two campaigns with intersecting set-pieces:
- Redemption Campaign (Cameleon + Zero)
- Corruption Campaign (Devil + Eclipse)

## Act I — Inciting Events
- Hit Gone Wrong: Cameleon discovers Meridan trafficking implicating Devil.
- Orders from 554567: Zero is sent to eliminate Cameleon; learns about parallel Earth interference.
- First Crossing: A breach links Prime and 554567 cells; uneasy truce in the field.

Decision Checkpoint A (Redemption): Spare a whistleblower or silence to protect cover.

## Act II — Rising Conflict
- Social Stealth Ops: infiltrations expose an MCB conspiracy masking Meridan instability.
- Devil Ascends: public attacks framed as insurgent acts; Greed’s influence expands.
- Eclipse’s Lesson: illusions test Zero’s loyalty; truth-bending memory arena.

Decision Checkpoint B (Dual): Choose civilians’ safety over perfect intel, or vice versa.

## Act III — Revelations
- Betrayal Unveiled: Devil admits using Cameleon’s past hits to power Greed’s rituals.
- Zero’s Choice: defy Eclipse or double down for mastery.
- Fractured Alliances: co-op splits; mirrored missions with asymmetrical objectives.

Decision Checkpoint C (Corruption): Consume a Meridan core for overwhelming power or destroy it.

## Climaxes
- Cameleon vs Devil: collapsing city; Meridan surges, civilian panic, timed rescues, and arena phase shifts.
- Zero vs Eclipse: illusion chamber; shadow clones, geometry shifts, and memory trial.

## Endings
- Redemption Ending: Cameleon sacrifices resonance to stabilize breaches; Zero frees captives; Greed retreats.
- Reform Ending: Devil breaks pact at 1 HP moment; Eclipse vanishes; cities stabilize.
- Ambiguous Ending: Cameleon disappears under a new identity; Zero rejects both masters; breaches persist.
- Corruption Ending: Devil enthrones as energy tyrant; Eclipse becomes Worldshade; Earths begin to merge.

## Post-Credits Hooks
- A hollow blade whispers; Greed seeks a new host.
- A child in 554567 imitates Zero flawlessly.
